Dr. Shalini Verma

Clinical Hypnotherapist & Psychotherapist

My philosophy is simple: sessions continue until breakthrough, not until the clock runs out. Our average session runs about 3 hours because real healing doesn't happen in neat 50-minute increments. Around 60% of my patients work with me online, connecting from across India and around the world. Distance has never been a barrier to deep, meaningful healing work.

In 2025, I was featured on the cover of Industry Insights Magazine as one of India's top 10 recommended psychotherapists — a recognition I carry with gratitude and a renewed commitment to this work.
